FILIP MALISZEWSKI

I took on the role of lead photographer in several fashion photoshoots during my studies at the Gerrit Rietveld Academie. These shoots were initiated by models and designers, and my responsibilities went beyond photography â€” I also acted as a visual director, translating their ideas into cohesive visual narratives.
The most notable project was a 2024 photoshoot with Polish drag queen Cosmia Absurd, marking her return after a year-long break for her transition. The goal was to make a bold impact on social media - a shoot that felt serious, intentional, and striking. Cosmia approached me with a list of references, the most memorable being a still of Fiona Apple with an octopus on her head from the Every Single Night music video. Together, we developed the concept, chose the wardrobe and makeup, and landed on a slimy, dark green color palette to set the tone. I also assembled a small team, selecting people based on their specific strengths to help bring the vision to life. To this day, this remains one of my biggest and proudest shoots, not only because of its scale, but because it showcased my work to thousands of people online, and reflected the full range of my creative direction and photographic style.
