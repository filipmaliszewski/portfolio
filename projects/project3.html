FILIP MALISZEWSKI

Skwarki Magazine was a student project from 2022, during my time in Willem De Kooning Academie in Rotterdam. It was made in collaboration with Zuzanna Miszkurka (advertising department) and Zuzanna Krzos (graphic design department). The topic that we agreed on was widely-understood erotica and experimentation with various mediums printing/photography/design etc. The main goal was to create a fun and playful magazine in the 2-weeks window between assignments. The work heavily focused on team effort and collaboration. As the person who had the most experience with cameras and lighting techniques, I lead the team in the photoshoots and thought them various techniques of how to properly use flash, blends and lamps. The few texts within the magazine are also my poetic addition to the effort. On the other hand I learned various thing from girls that were focal in my later works. The importance in choice of paper, printing on various materials - such as fabric or meat! or something as simple as the importance of deadlines and scheduling when in collaborative projects. Overall I was happy to give my queer angle within this project and find an outlet for the mediums that I ignored before.
